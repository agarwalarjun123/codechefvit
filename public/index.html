About Us

About CodeChef

CodeChef is a competitive programming website. It is a non-profit educational initiative of Directi aimed at providing a platform for students, young software professionals to practice, hone their programming skills through online contests.Apart from this, the 'CodeChef For Schools' program aims to reach out to young students and inculcate a culture of programming in Indian schools

About CodeChef VIT Chapter

In Vellore Institute of technology,Codechef is a technical chapter that helps students all over the campus to improve their coding skills.The core commitee members of the chapters are divided into three areas of expertise-Technical,Management and Design.The main objective is to provide the students a platform where they can learn,share and test their knowledge.
